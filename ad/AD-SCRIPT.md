# Vinted Brand Dashboard — 10-Second Ad Script

**Audience:** investors + customers (dual) · **Format:** vertical 9:16, 1080×1920, ~10s, captions-first, soft music bed.

## Why this format (research-backed)
- **Hook → Value → CTA**, compressed to 10s. First 3s is the only guaranteed attention window.
- Lead with a **specific outcome**, not features ("specificity beats vagueness").
- Dual-audience line does double duty: customers hear "no more spreadsheets," investors hear "automated revenue tracking."
- Vertical + captions (most people watch muted); 9–15s is the proven sweet spot for short ads.

## Shot-by-shot

| Time | On-screen (caption) | Visual | Voiceover (optional) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0.0–3.0s | **FOR LUXURY RESELLERS** / **Reselling bags?** / **Stop guessing.** | Dark screen, gold accent bar, bold headline | *"Running a bag business?"* |
| 3.0–6.6s | **FROM SOURCED TO SOLD** + the 4 stage labels appear in sequence: **SOURCED** (blue) → **IN TRANSIT** (amber) → **IN STOCK** (green) → **SOLD** (grey); then **€2,847 net profit this month** counts up in gold | Stage flow + profit ticking up | *"Track every bag from sourced to sold — sales detected automatically."* |
| 6.6–8.6s | **Every bag, every euro** / **tracked automatically.** | Value line | *"and your profit, in real time."* |
| 8.6–10.2s | **VINTED** / **BRAND DASHBOARD** / *Run resale like a business.* | Gold logo lock-up + tagline | *"Run resale like a business."* |

**The line that carries the most weight:** *"Every bag and every euro — tracked automatically."*
