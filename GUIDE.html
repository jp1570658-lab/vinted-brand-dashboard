Vinted Brand Dashboard

A mobile‑first command centre for a luxury second‑hand bag reselling business. It tracks every bag from the moment you source it abroad to the moment it sells on Vinted — and automates the boring parts: detecting sales, logging expenses, forecasting revenue, and warning you when stock gets stuck.

DEMO ready Live on Railway

🧭 What it is

One screen that answers: what do I own, where is it, what's stuck, and am I making money?

📦 Inventory tracker

Every bag is a record moving through four stages, with photos, grades, costs, and a runner (the person who sourced it).

🤖 Automation

Sales are detected automatically by scraping your Vinted wardrobe; expenses pull in from Wise; aging stock triggers alerts.

📈 Intelligence

KPIs, a profit‑analytics page, and Claude‑powered pricing, sourcing‑ROI and revenue forecasts.

Built with — Node + Express + TypeScript and Prisma on the backend; React + TypeScript + Tailwind (Vite) on the frontend; PostgreSQL in production. Everything runs end‑to‑end in DEMO mode with zero credentials before you ever connect a real account.

🔄 The 4‑stage lifecycle

Every item lives in exactly one stage. Moving an item stamps the date it entered that stage, so timers and analytics are automatic.

SOURCED

Bought abroad, not yet shipped. Capital is committed. This is where the 14‑day aging clock runs.

→
IN TRANSIT

On its way to you. Shows days in transit.

→
IN STOCK

In hand and listed on Vinted. Has a listed price you can quick‑edit.

→
SOLD

Sold. Net profit & margin are calculated and frozen on the record.

Why stages matter — each list page in the app is just a filtered view of one stage, and the "days in stage" timers (and the aging alert) are derived from the stamped timestamps.

🔐 Logging in & modes

The whole app sits behind one password. There's no multi‑user system — it's a single‑operator tool.

  • Login: a single password (DASHBOARD_PASSWORD). A session cookie keeps you logged in for 7 days.
  • Mode badge: the top bar shows DEMO or LIVE so you always know whether you're looking at mock data or the real thing.

DEMO mode

Every external call — Vinted, Wise, Claude, email — is replaced by realistic mock data. The database is seeded with demo items and runners. Perfect for learning the app or testing changes. Default password in demo: demo1234.

LIVE mode

Real APIs and your real database. Switched on with APP_MODE=LIVE plus the relevant credentials. Background jobs (scraper, Wise poll, alert email) only run in LIVE.

➕ Adding stock — the Quick Intake

The floating + button (bottom‑right, on every screen) opens the Quick Intake sheet. Designed to add a bag in seconds from your phone.

  1. Add a photo — two options: 📷 Take photo opens your camera directly, or 🖼️ Choose from gallery opens your photo library / files. After picking one, the buttons become Retake photo / Change.
  2. Brand & Model — e.g. Louis Vuitton · Neverfull MM. Both required.
  3. Grade — tap a chip: A / B / C / Ungraded.
  4. Purchase price + currency — type the amount and pick EUR, GBP, NGN or KES. It's converted to EUR and frozen on the record (see Money & profit maths).
  5. Runner — choose an existing runner, or type a new name to create one inline.
  6. Notes — optional.

On save, the item is created as SOURCED, time‑stamped, and appears immediately in Future Stock.

The camera vs. gallery fix — previously the photo field forced the camera on mobile (it used a "capture" hint). It's now two separate inputs so you can pull an existing photo from your gallery or shoot a new one.

⏰ Aging alerts

Capital stuck abroad is the silent killer of a reselling business, so the app watches for it.

  • Any SOURCED item older than 14 days is "aged".
  • Its card gets a red border + a pulsing ⚠️ badge showing how long it's been stuck.
  • A dismissible banner appears at the top: "⚠️ N bags have been stuck abroad for over 14 days." (Dismissal lasts the session.)
  • In LIVE mode a daily 08:00 email lists every flagged item with its runner, days stuck, and value at risk.

🛰️ How sale detection works

You shouldn't have to mark things sold by hand. The app figures it out by watching your Vinted wardrobe.

Wardrobe‑diff scraping (primary)

  • Once a day at 06:00 the app fetches your public Vinted wardrobe (your live listings).
  • It compares today's listings to the snapshot from the last run. A listing that has vanished is treated as sold.
  • Each detected sale is matched to an IN STOCK item by name similarity (Levenshtein distance). A confident match is marked SOLD with source AUTO_VINTED; anything it can't match is logged for manual review rather than guessed.
Safety guard — if the wardrobe fetch returns zero listings (usually an expired cookie or a blocked request), the run aborts. A bad fetch will never mark your whole wardrobe as sold.

Gmail detection (fallback)

An earlier method parsed Vinted "You've sold" emails from Gmail and matched them to items (source AUTO_GMAIL). It's kept as a manual fallback (/api/sync/gmail) but no longer runs automatically — the wardrobe scraper replaced it.

Manual

You can always change an item's status yourself; manually sold items are tagged MANUAL. You can also trigger either sync on demand from Settings.

🧾 Expense tracking (Wise)

Costs flow in from your Wise account so profit reflects reality, not just sale price minus purchase price.

  • In LIVE mode the app polls Wise every 6 hours for outgoing payments, de‑duplicated by Wise's own transaction id (no double‑counting).
  • Each transaction is auto‑categorised: courier names (DHL / UPS / Colissimo) → Shipping; a runner's name → Runner payment; large transfers → Stock purchase; otherwise Other.
  • On the Transactions page you can link a cost to a specific bag — it then shows on that item's detail and feeds its net‑profit calculation.

✨ AI insights

Three Claude‑powered analyses (model claude-haiku-4-5). They unlock once you have at least 5 sold items — below that you get a friendly "not enough data yet" message instead of a guess.

💷 Time‑to‑sell & pricing

For a given item, suggests a recommended price and expected days‑to‑sell with a confidence level and reasoning, based on your last 90 days of comparable sales.

🌍 Sourcing ROI

Ranks your runners by margin, speed and total profit, and names the one to prioritise this week.

🔮 Revenue forecast

Projects next month's revenue and profit from recent weekly sales and current inventory, flagging risks like aged stock.

In DEMO mode these return realistic canned responses so you can see the panels without an API key. Token usage is logged server‑side in LIVE mode.

💶 Money & profit maths

All figures display in EUR. Conversions are deliberately simple and frozen so historical numbers never drift.

Currency conversion

There's no live FX feed. A fixed rate table converts the purchase price to EUR at the moment you add the item, and that EUR value is stored on the record permanently.

CurrencyRate → EUR
EUR1.00
GBP1.17
NGN0.00055
KES0.0069

Profit & margin

net profit = sale price − ( purchase price in EUR + shipping + customs )
margin %   = net profit ÷ sale price × 100

Missing costs count as zero. Net profit is recalculated and stored whenever an item becomes SOLD or its sale‑related figures change. Inventory value (a dashboard KPI) is the total EUR purchase cost of everything not yet sold — i.e. capital currently tied up.

🤖 Automation schedule

Background jobs run only in LIVE mode. In DEMO they're disabled (so seeded stock isn't repeatedly "sold") — use the manual triggers in Settings instead.

JobWhenWhat it does
Vinted wardrobe scrapeDaily 06:00 CRON_TZDiffs your wardrobe to detect & record sales.
Wise syncEvery 6 hoursImports & categorises new outgoing payments.
Aging checkDaily 08:00Emails an alert if any SOURCED item is > 14 days old.
Timezone — cron fires in the server's timezone. Hosts like Railway run in UTC, so set CRON_TZ to your IANA zone (e.g. Europe/Amsterdam) to make "06:00" mean your local 6 AM.

🛡️ Security

  • Single‑password gate on the whole API (only /api/health and login are public).
  • Session cookies: httpOnly, sameSite: strict, and secure in production; 7‑day lifetime.
  • Rate limiting: 100 requests per 15 minutes per IP on /api (returns 429 beyond that).
  • Helmet security headers, gzip compression, and input validation on writes.
  • Secrets are read from environment variables only — never hard‑coded or committed.

🩺 Troubleshooting

The AI panels say "not enough data yet"

You need at least 5 SOLD items before the pricing, sourcing and forecast features turn on.

Nothing got marked sold overnight

In LIVE the scrape runs at 06:00 in CRON_TZ. If the run found 0 listings it aborts on purpose (likely an expired VINTED_COOKIE) — refresh the cookie and trigger a manual sync from Settings.

A sale was detected but not matched to an item

Name similarity didn't clear the threshold (common when two bags share a brand/model). It's logged for manual review — just set that item to SOLD yourself.

My profit looks off

Net profit subtracts shipping and customs too. Make sure those costs are filled in (or linked from Wise). Currency is frozen at intake, so a later rate change won't retroactively adjust old items — by design.

I'm getting 429 errors

That's the rate limiter — 100 requests / 15 min per IP. Wait a moment and retry.
